Shiritori is a Japanese word game in which the players are required to say a word which begins with the final kana of the previous word
  • A random word will be chosen as the starting word
  • Only nouns are permitted.
  • Words may not be repeated
  • A player who plays a word ending in the mora "N" (ん) loses the game, as no Japanese word begins with that character.
  • Phrases connected by no (の; meaning roughly "of") are permitted, but only in those cases where the phrase is sufficiently fossilized to be considered a "word".
